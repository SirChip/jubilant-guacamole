Amorim's Relief: United Boss Praises Fight After Burnley Win

Posted on October 28, 2023

Manchester United laboured to a 3-2 victory over Burnley at Old Trafford, a win that relieved pressure on manager Ruben Amorim but highlighted familiar issues. Here's what the boss and his players had to say after the final whistle...

The Manager's Take

Ruben Amorim was clear in his assessment: "It is three points, if you look at the game we should solve it in the first half and control the game - but we are fighting to the last minute because of things we overcomplicate."

He also provided a cryptic update on injuries: "I don't know. We will check them. We need those guys really bad to be competitive," he said regarding Cunha and Mount.
