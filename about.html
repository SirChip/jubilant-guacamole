About United Press

Welcome to United Press, your dedicated source for comprehensive coverage of Manchester United FC.

Frustrated by the scattered nature of post-match quotes across social media, we started this site to do one thing well: consolidate the post-match reactions from the manager and players into clean, readable, and well-structured articles.

Our mission is to save you time and give you the full context of what's being said at Old Trafford, straight from the source.

This site is a fan project and is not officially affiliated with Manchester United Football Club.
